Our Story

MogoBiz is the brainchild of Ebiznext, a French tech consultancy company that specializes in devops, big data, UX, cloud and agile solutions developed on the Java or .Net platforms. After years of resolving customer issues, we selected some of our hottest modules, revved them up in Scala, added a REST API and top notch caching systems, and combined them all to create MogoBiz, a high performance, highly scalable ecommerce platform.

With MogoBiz, we aim to accelerate the growth of online and mobile businesses. We do this by offering the best ecommerce solution at a fraction of the cost of other such offerings.

Our solution extends full creative potential to our customers with intuitive site construction over multiple channels that is modular and adaptable to their needs.
We are aware of business needs and stay aware by maintaining close relationships with our customers. Face time is important to us. Integrity is important to us. Transparency, too. Fluff and empty promises are not us.

We pride ourselves on our technical excellence. Our platform is created with the best, latest and most performing technology for fast response time, almost instantaneous upload and augmented analytics.

We strongly believe that MogoBiz delivers what people in the ecommerce field have been awaiting for a long time: truly agile ecommerce web sites.
