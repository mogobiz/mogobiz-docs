MogoBiz B2B Commerce Features

MogoBiz is working towards a complete B2B and B2C2B solution, in addition to the rich-feature set currently available in the B2C Commerce solution.
  • The B2B-specific features that are currently available are:
  • Multi-currency, Multi-language, Multi-taxation
  • Bulk discounts
  • Minimum price/order for bulk orders
  • Credit card and PayPal payment options
  • Integration with 3rd party tools (commercial or in-house solutions), such as ERP, CRM, billing & invoicing solutions
